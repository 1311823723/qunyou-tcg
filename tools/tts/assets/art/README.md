# TTS art assets

These images are used only by the local Tabletop Simulator export pipeline.

Current mappings are defined in `tools/tts/export-tts.js`:

- `keke-assassin.png`: normal Keke assassin art for `body_mizai_001` and `char_aggro_001`.
- `keke-assassin-mega.png`: Mega-style Keke art for the back face of `body_mizai_001`.
- `baizi-body.png`: normal Baizi body art for `body_trans_001`.
- `baizi-body-mega-v2.png`: Mega-style Baizi art for the back face of `body_trans_001`.
- `guamao-body.png`: normal Guamao body art for `body_combo_001`.
- `guamao-body-mega.png`: Mega-style Guamao art for the back face of `body_combo_001`.
- `xiangcai-prophet-final-v2.png`: Prophet Xiangcai art for `char_combo_001`.
- `xiangcai-politician-final.png`: Politician Xiangcai art for `char_combo_004`.
- `xiangcai-watcher-final-v2.png`: Watcher Xiangcai art for `char_combo_007`.
- `xiangcai-neo-final-v2.png`: Neo Xiangcai art for `char_combo_013`.

Card data remains in `data/cards/*.json`; art mapping is export-only.
